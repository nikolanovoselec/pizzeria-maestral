Our story

A family, a flame,
and a very small kitchen.

In 1987, Ante and Marija opened a six-table room just off the Kalelarga with a borrowed oven and a recipe from Ante's grandmother in the hills behind Zadar. The pizza was simple, the tomatoes were real, and the regulars never left.

Today their children run the pass and their grandchildren clear the tables, but nothing important has changed. The dough still rises overnight. The mozzarella still comes from the same farm across the channel. And the oak still crackles every evening as the sun drops behind the islands.

- The Maretic family
